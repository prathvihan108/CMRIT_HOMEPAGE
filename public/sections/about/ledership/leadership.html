President's Message

The CMR Jnanadhara Trust was established in 1991 to deliver the highest quality of education to one and all. With this view, the CMR educational institutes were founded, to give every student a chance at an outstanding, value-based and well-rounded education. We began with only a handful of kindergarten students when the CMR National Public School first opened its doors nearly two decades ago, but due to the dedicated services of the Trust, to the community, we have grown tremendously since then. Today, CMR Group of Institutions consists of 19 institutions in India and abroad, 69 different academic programmes, and over 900 faculty members. Every year, more than 15000 students including international students from over 58 countries study in our classrooms. I am proud to say that our students and alumni are enthusiastic, dedicated learners and high achievers. Each CMR campus is equipped with the best of infrastructure: well-stocked libraries, wireless internet everywhere, state of the art laboratories, comfortable, secure hostel accommodation, several world class auditoriums and sports facilities.

Dr. Sanjay Jain, Principal

Learning is ‘fun’ at CMRIT, as students and faculty members get together to make a difference through persevering and achieving intellectual satisfaction. We emphasise the motto “Learning to Learn”, during the students’ sojourn here, when ‘learning’ becomes a lifelong practice. Several prestigious international and national awards garnered by students and faculty epitomizes CMRIT as an eminent engineering institute. The unique ‘Maker Space’ on campus, the first of its kind in Bengaluru, imbibes design thinking, where students experiment with ideas and build prototypes. Over the years, students have worked and created several useful products and solutions addressing the pain-points of society. CMRIT has several jewels in its crown: an award by the President of India for an innovative and frugal technique to diagnose infectious diseases; an international medal for outstanding contributions to advance material science and technology; fellow titles from Stanford University; training for students at Google Mountain View, USA; an award at Microsoft Vfest; an award at Texas Instruments Challenge; awards by IEEE; a Portuguese innovation medal; a gold medal at the India International Innovation Fair; as well as several top ranks in the university amongst other achievements.

Dr. B. Narasimha Murthy, Vice-Principal

CMR Institute of Technology (CMRIT), established in the year 2000 with a vision to be a globally recognized institution to provide high-quality technical and management education, believes that students are the torchbearers of every nation. In order to bring in positive change towards a sustainable future, we ensure all round development of students by inculcating active participation in various technical and non-technical clubs that provide ample opportunities for gaining practical knowledge and industry skills; in tandem with the conventional academic curriculum. We encourage hands-on learning through major/ mini interdisciplinary projects with support of our state-of-the-art Makerspace laboratory that houses CNC machines, laser cutter, 3D printing tools, Centres of Excellence and research labs. Many of our alumni are at top positions globally and are actively involved in imparting project experiences to students. Our non-technical clubs include the likes of sports, music, dance, and the Kannada club. These activities provide students various pathways to widen their horizons beyond academics enabling them to create a niche in today’s fast-changing and competitive world. CMRIT is also determined to serve the rural community by adopting villages to provide technical solutions to their real-time problems. Our students and faculty are also actively involved in this mission. To encourage leadership and team spirit amongst students, we conduct a number of national and international conferences, seminars and workshops.
