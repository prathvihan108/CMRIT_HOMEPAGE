COLLEGE INTERNAL COMPLAINTS COMMITTEE(CICC)

Purpose of CICC

CMRIT believes that the principles of justice, humanity and dignity should be dispensed irrespective of caste, creed, social status and gender of a person. Therefore in keeping with the ethos of this institution and in acquiescence with the instructions of National Commission for Women and the directives of Hon’ble Supreme Court Judgment (Attachment 1) on the subject of sexual harassment of women in the workplace, an Internal Complaints Committee was established for taking into account the complaints of sexual harassment of women staff and students.

Procedure for filing a Complaint

  • Complaints regarding sexual harassment must be mailed to the chairperson or submitted in written (legible handwriting) form on paper.
  • The nature of the complaint should be clearly stated in detail, including dates and locations of the incidents.
  • As per The Act, complaints must be made within three months from the date of the incident, or from the date of the last incident in case of a series.
  • The complaint must not be anonymous, and the aggrieved person’s name and address should be legible.
  • According to the Sexual Harassment of Women at Workplace (Prevention, Prohibition and Redressal) Rules, 2013, if the aggrieved woman is unable to file a complaint due to physical incapacity, a co-worker or any person with knowledge of the incident can file on her behalf with her written consent.
  • The written complaint should be handed over to any member of the CICC.
